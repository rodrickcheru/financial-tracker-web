Your Financial Overview

Managing your finances can often feel overwhelming. Here's a snapshot of how your finances can look:

Example Scenario: Young Professional

A young professional, Jane, earns KSh 80,000 a month. After rent, utilities, and groceries, she has KSh 40,000 left for savings and discretionary spending. With our financial tracker, she categorized her expenses:

  • Rent: KSh 30,000
  • Utilities: KSh 5,000
  • Groceries: KSh 10,000
  • Entertainment: KSh 5,000

She is on track to save KSh 20,000 each month towards her emergency fund.

Example Scenario: Family Budgeting

The Ndung'u family earns a combined KSh 150,000 a month. They track their expenses carefully, ensuring they allocate funds to necessities while saving for their children's education.

  • Mortgage: KSh 50,000
  • Childcare: KSh 20,000
  • Food: KSh 25,000
  • Savings for Education: KSh 15,000

This careful budgeting allows them to enjoy family outings without financial stress.
