Reports & Analytics

Generating reports is a key part of understanding your financial health. Consider these scenarios:

Example Scenario: Monthly Review

Every month, Chris reviews his spending. He categorizes his expenses and compares them to his budget:

  • Budgeted Expenses: KSh 50,000
  • Actual Expenses: KSh 45,000

This analysis helps him adjust his budget for the next month.

Example Scenario: Yearly Goals

Susan sets yearly financial goals and tracks her progress:

  • Goal: Save KSh 300,000 by the end of the year
  • Current Savings: KSh 200,000
  • Monthly Savings Target: KSh 8,000

By generating reports, she stays accountable and motivated to reach her goal.
