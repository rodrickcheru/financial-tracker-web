Example Scenario: Newly Married Couple

John and Mary recently got married and decided to create a joint budget:

  • Combined Income: KSh 120,000
  • Housing: KSh 40,000
  • Food: KSh 20,000
  • Transportation: KSh 15,000
  • Entertainment: KSh 10,000
  • Savings: KSh 25,000

This budgeting method helps them save for their future while enjoying their present.

Example Scenario: Retired Individual

Linda, a retiree, has a fixed income of KSh 70,000 per month from her pension. She budgets carefully to stretch her funds:

  • Healthcare: KSh 15,000
  • Housing: KSh 25,000
  • Groceries: KSh 10,000
  • Leisure Activities: KSh 5,000
  • Savings: KSh 15,000

With her budget, she can enjoy life without financial stress.
